ABOUT THE ONES RESTAURANT

The Ones Restaurant is an online Filipino food restaurant that offers various cuisines such as meal, appetizer, pasta, noodle, and beverage. It was founded on April 26, 2020 in Batangas City, Philippines by our Filipino owner Kristina Antenor when the coronavirus outbreak hit the country and the government issued an enhanced community quarantine.
