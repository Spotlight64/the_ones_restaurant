Frequently Asked Questions

How did your business started?

Cooking is my passion since I was a kid but I have other business to work on so I just cook for myself, my family/relatives and friends when there is an occasion. I started the food business online after coronavirus hit us and the government issued an enhanced community quarantine. I was looking for something that I get to earn and also showed people that I have qualities at cooking great foods, clean, delicious and healthy.

What is your best selling product?

My best selling product would always be pork barbecue, sinaing na tulingan (Batangas delicacy), spaghetti (Italian style), tuna pasta, lasagna, seafood kare kare, and pork sausage. Other foods are best selling as well but I would suggest you to try these foods first that I have masterized throughout the years.
